My Favourite Pet

My family and I love raising fish, and we have several aquariums and a fish pond at home. I cherish the memory of my fish named Se, which I raised for three years. Unfortunately, I cannot raise furry animals due to eczema, but I find joy in taking care of aquatic pets like fish and turtles. Ada cerita disebalik nama "Se", dimana saya menamakan se bagi menunjukkan seekor jadi memudahkan saya untuk menamakan ikan saya dengan Se. This is the longest fish I have kept. The routine I always do is every 2 weeks, I will clean the aquarium. Feed every morning and night.I was very sad when I found out he was gone because this fish was very special to me.
