My hobby


In my free time, my favorite activity is cycling. I really like cycling because riding a bike increases my stamina. By cycling I can see the green plants around me. I can discover and explore the beauty of flora and fauna in Malaysia. The place I often cycle is in Putrajaya. Putrajaya is a place with less traffic compared to Kuala Lumpur, and the beauty of the plants there is also well maintained.
